Established: 12 July 2016 under the Aadhaar Act, 2016.

Purpose: UIDAI is responsible for issuing a 12-digit unique identification number (Aadhaar) to all residents of India, based on their biometric and demographic data. Aadhaar serves as a proof of identity and address, facilitating access to various government services and subsidies.

Key Services:

  • Enrollment for Aadhaar
  • Updating Aadhaar details
  • Downloading e-Aadhaar
  • Verification of Aadhaar number
  • Virtual ID (VID) generation
